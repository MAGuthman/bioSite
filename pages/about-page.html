Mother. Student. Editor.

Sara Guthman takes on many hats and at just twenty-seven years old, has accomplished much in her life. From becoming a mother at age twenty-four, to starting her own business at twenty-six, Sara is on the go, always working towards the next goal. She is a true inspiration for her friends and family.

Early Years

Sara grew up in an average household with her two parents, one older sister and one younger sister. As a child, Sara was a shy individual with a creative spirit and a love for reading. Her parents often joke that most nights in the crib were spent with indestructible books in lieu of stuffed animals or plush toys. Throughout her childhood one of her favorite past-times was to curl up with a good book and block out the world.

Family

Sara attended one of the most diverse high schools in her state. This is where it all started. It was here, in a random sophomore science class that she met the love of her life, Daniel. The young couple were quick friends, bonding on similar interests and goals for a future life. After some time as close friends, the couple became inseparable and soon become the ideal definition of high school sweethearts. Danny moved on to become an Insurance Agent while Sara attended cosmetology school. In 2019, the happy couple welcomed their first-born son, and currently only child, into the world. They were married soon thereafter. Being a mother is one of Sara’s greatest joys in life. Sara migrated to working full-time as a stay-at-home mother. She often comments about how proud she is that her son shows the same interests, and promising future as an avid reader.

Career

Education has always been important to Sara but has not always been able to be the top priority. After finishing cosmetology school, Sara found that it was no longer her path, and left it behind. However, recently Sara has found a renewed vigor for school, going back to put herself on a new path to success. Attending classes while simultaneously caring for her son, Sara became a college graduate earning her Associates in Arts in May of 2022. She is now working towards her Bachelor’s in English and will eventually move on to earn her Master’s. Being a full-time mother and a full-time student did not leave her with much free time. That did not stop her from using what little time she had to start FoxTale Editing – her very own editing company for intendent authors. Sara is looking to eventually break into the world of publishing, essentially turning her lifelong passion of reading into a lifelong career.
